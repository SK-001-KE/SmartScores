About SmartScores

SmartScores is a simple, efficient and mobile-friendly app designed to help teachers record, analyze and track their mean scores effortlessly — even offline!

Key Features

  • 📊 Save and review teacher mean scores by subject, grade, and stream
  • 📱 Works offline and can be installed as an app (PWA)
  • 📈 Generates automatic term summaries and AI insights
  • 🧠 Smart grading using Exceeding / Meeting / Approaching / Below expectations
  • 🧾 Export data or download printable PDF reports
